General

What is the Broadwings Family Office? +
Broadwings Family Office is a boutique multi-family office offering holistic, personalized services to ultra-high-net-worth individuals, NRIs, entrepreneurs, and legacy-driven families. From wealth management and succession planning to lifestyle concierge and philanthropic advisory, we provide 360° solutions tailored to your financial and personal aspirations.
Who typically partners with Broadwings? +
Our clients include multi-generational business families, globally mobile NRIs, successful entrepreneurs, and promoters seeking structured, long-term strategies to preserve, grow, and transition their wealth.
Where is Broadwings Family Office based? +
Broadwings is headquartered in India and works with families globally, including clients with assets and family members across North America, the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Wealth & Investment Advisory

What does your wealth management service include? +
Our wealth management covers holistic asset allocation, bespoke portfolio construction, estate planning, succession, and liquidity strategies — all aligned with your long-term legacy goals.
Do you offer investment advisory across asset classes? +
Yes. We advise across equity, debt, alternatives (private equity, VC), and impact investing, ensuring each asset fits your family's values and objectives.

Estate & Legacy Planning

Why is estate planning important? +
Proper estate planning ensures your wealth is distributed as intended, avoids legal complications, and secures your legacy across generations. We help you structure wills, trusts, and succession frameworks seamlessly.
Can you help prepare the next generation? +
Absolutely. We offer next-gen mentoring, governance education, and financial literacy programs to empower future custodians of your wealth.
⚖️

Legal & Tax Advisory

How does Broadwings support legal structuring? +
We assist with family constitutions, shareholder agreements, trust formation, and legal due diligence — offering proactive legal counsel aligned with your family's interests.
What kind of tax services do you provide? +
From domestic to international tax planning, capital gains optimization, gift and inheritance tax strategies to regulatory filings (FEMA, FATCA, CRS), we deliver end-to-end tax advisory.
👥

Family Governance

What is family governance, and do I need it? +
Family governance defines how decisions are made within the family and business. It includes family constitutions, councils, and succession structures — essential to avoid future disputes and maintain unity.
Do you help with conflict resolution? +
Yes. We establish preventive frameworks and offer facilitation to manage intergenerational or business-related conflicts peacefully and constructively.
🏠

Real Estate Solutions

What types of real estate services do you offer? +

Can you help with international property investments? +
Yes. We support both acquisition and compliance for residential and commercial properties across global markets.
